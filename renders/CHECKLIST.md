# Render checklist — visually verifiable facts

Every generated render is checked item-by-item against the relevant list below.

**Pass/fail rule:** an item **fails only if the image visibly CONTRADICTS it.** If a view simply doesn't
show something (e.g. the north garage end isn't in frame), mark it **N/A — absence is not a violation.**
An item passes when it's visible and correct. Fix + regenerate only on real contradictions.

Derived from `docs/` (source of truth). See `brief.md` for the design rationale.

## Orientation (checkable in the photo — no diagram needed)

Northern hemisphere: the **sun-facing side just IS south**. So orientation is correct *in the photo* when
the light falls the right way — you don't need a compass or a separate diagram, just consistent sun.

- [ ] **Main sunlight lands on the SOLAR ROOF + GLASS façade** (the sun-facing = south side). The solid
      **garage/north end is the shadier, less-lit side.** A visible reversal (glass/solar in shadow while
      the solid wall is sunlit) is the only real fail here.
- [ ] **Sun reads HIGH / midday**, or at least clearly on the solar-glass side — not a low side/back sun
      raking across that leaves the panels off the main light (that's what made an earlier render look
      "west").
- [ ] **Mono-pitch roof slopes toward the glass/sun side** (high at the solid/garage end, low toward the
      glass). N/A if the slope direction isn't legible from the chosen angle.

## Exterior (south hero)

- [ ] **Solar array covers the whole south-facing roof slope** (the dominant, sun-facing surface).
- [ ] **South + east façade is floor-to-ceiling glass** curtain wall over **2–3 internal levels**, with an
